Sandeep Kulkarni
This place is hard to find but once you’re in you get the feeling you’ve entered a high ceilinged industrial structure which got hurriedly converted into a café. The ambiance is pretty informal and this looks like a place at which you could spend hours with a drink and a book. We went for the fries, veg. burger and the smoked salmon. The fries were mercifully crisp served with a lovely dip, but the patty of the veg burger was quiet sad (especially since we expected a lot from it), the smoked salmon looked served right out of the can and was far from special. Another place whose interiors impressed me more than the food.
